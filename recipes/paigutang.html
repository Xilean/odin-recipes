Pai Gu Tang

Description

A bone broth soup stewed with daikon and ginger.

Ingredients

  • Short ribs (pork or lamb)
  • Daikon
  • Goji berries
  • Ginger
  • Scallions
  • Cilantro
  1. Blanch the short ribs in boiling water for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Remove water with skimmed fats and replace with clean pot of water.
  3. Stew short ribs with ginger and scallions for 1-2 hours.
